Internet History

Posted on October 30, 2021 by Now-U-Know. Images from CreativeCommons

The inception of the internet dates back to the 1950's. It is one of the most precious inventions of all time. The concept of the inter connects people all around the world with an unlimited amount of knowledge to share.

No invention in history has grown so quick to touch so many lives. Within a small span of 30 years the internet was commercialized and laid a foundation for the advancement of futuristic technology. Some of which include machine learning, data mining, robotics, artificial intelligence automobiles, surgical robots, astrology, etc.
