Napster

In 1999, Napster was founded by Shawn Fanning .It was a pioneer in peer-to-peer file sharing Internet software,especially digital audio files.It allowed the users to share and download the music that was copyrighted,which is illegal . Napster faced a lot of criticism and the regulators shut it down due to the illegal file-sharing.But the initiative that Napster provided , had already created a movement which allowed the users across the world to connect over the internet and form groups.This is a step stone to social networking.In 2001,Napster shutdown its entire network.

Facebook & Twitter

Mark Zuckerberg along with his fellow Harvard College students and roommates Eduardo Saverin, Andrew McCollum, Dustin Moskovitz, and Chris Hughes, founded Facebook in 2004.Today Facebook, attracts millions of users worldwide by offering interactive social communications.They help family and friends stay connected in even adverse times like the recent pandemic
Twitter , is a micro blogging and social networking site was created by Jack Dorsey, Noah Glass, Biz Stone, and Evan Williams in March 2006 and launched in July of that year. It is estimated that around 186 million users are there on Twitter in the year 2020.
