EBAY

Ebay Inc, a multinational e-commerce corporation facilitates consumer-to-consumer and business-to-consumer sales through its website. It was founded by Pierre Omidyar in 1995.eBay is a multibillion-dollar business with operations in about 32 countries.It manages the eBay website, an online auction and shopping website in which people and businesses buy and sell a wide variety of goods and services worldwide. The buyers can use it for , but the sellers have to pay to list their

items after a limited period.Back in 1995, when Bezos was shipping books from his garage, Pierre Omidyar, a software programmer, started coding a simple Web site he called Auction Web. Omidyar was curious if people would use the Internet to bid on each other's used items. Looking around for something to sell, Omidyar picked up a broken laser pointer. Within a day, it had sold for $14.83 Omidyar e-mailed the buyer to make sure the guy knew it was broken. The buyer's response? "I'm a collector of broken laser pointers". eBay levelled the e-commerce playing field. You didn't have to be a Web entrepreneur or an existing business to sell things online.

Amazon

Jeff Bezos founded Amazon from his garage in Bellevue, Washington. Amazon.com set the standard for a customer-oriented e-commerce Web site. Users could search available titles by keyword, author or subject. They could browse books by category and even get personalized recommendations ,purchase books quickly and securely with the patented "one-click" checkout system.

Amazon went public in 1997.Amazon has expanded its offerings beyond books. Amazon distributes a variety of downloadable and streaming content through its Amazon Prime Video, Amazon Music, Twitch, and Audible subsidiaries.
